History and Current status

Our decision to establish Dithakga TV was intensely influenced by the industry needs, skills, background and passion we have for FTA Satellite TV, over the-top ( OTT) streaming & social media, television Production, Entertainment and advertising industry

Mission Statement

We want to play a part in the development and growth of the television production in South Africa as we brainstorm and experiment with innovative ideas that will forever change and shape the world within the sphere of the industry.

OBJECTIVES AND GOALS

Our goal is to create more than the average number of jobs with Knowledge intensive, involving highly skilled workers Our objective is to surround ourselves with permanent experienced, professional and courteous personnel who understand and respect the industry within a period of five years starting from 2019.

Our Values

Our customers are the core to every goal we intend to achieve, they are the blueprint and a stepping stone to everything we want to build, and hence they are a bigger part of the things we value. In addition, we value opinions and input that other players in the field of Media Industry make about our service.  Our values stream also flows to the suggestions that our clients make about the projects we implement.

Our Vision

To improve the state of humanity by contributing to the economy of South Africa.  To transfer skills to upcoming media personalities in order for them to start their businesses, this will be contributing to the growth of Media industry.

About Dithakga-TV

DithakgaTV

Dithakga TV is a new international Satellite Television station that came at a time when the Television industry is migrating from analog to digital. It is one of the Television Stations that represents the South African media industry. We broadcast the following

  • Movies
  • Talk shows
  • local Sports

  • Comedy shows
